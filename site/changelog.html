The evidence contract gets a gate that actually checks it

  • tests/fixtures/first-packet/ got real citation checksums, so every claim in the proof-of-shape packet is checked against the source it names, not just cross-referenced by eye.

Evidence: ci: gate boundary grep and packet fixture and test: add first-packet citation checksums on master.
